Prequalification

Knowing the difference between prequalification and preapproval will not only save you disappointment later, it can also save you from confusion.

It goes without saying that purchasing a home is an exiting time! On the other hand, it can also be a confusing time, particularly for a first time home buyer. However, not to worry. At Mortgage Sidekicks, we are here to help you along the part of your path, where things often start... prequalification.

The most important thing that should be cleared up is the difference between prequalification and preapproval. Knowing what the difference is between these two terms can save you fom disappointment. With prequalification, you are given an estimate of how much you might be able to be loaned. When it comes to preapproval, you are given notice that you have been approved for a set loan amount. And, this approved loan amount is given to you prior to selecting a property.

With that very important clarification made, you're ready to click on the "prequalify" button to start the first step towards buying your new home!
